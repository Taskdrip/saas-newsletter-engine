Build a production-grade, enterprise-level SaaS email newsletter and marketing platform named CampaignForge.

The application should use the open-source Listmonk project as its email campaign engine instead of reinventing email delivery.

GitHub Reference:

https://github.com/knadh/listmonk

The application should extend Listmonk into a true multi-tenant SaaS platform similar to Mailchimp, Brevo, ConvertKit, Beehiiv, or ActiveCampaign.

This must be a complete working production application, not a prototype.
